Mental Health Tools

Use these tools to track your mood and assess your mental health.

Sanvello

A comprehensive digital mental health platform offering mood tracking, CBT-based tools, guided journeys, and community support to help manage stress, anxiety, and depression.

Happify

Provides science-based activities, games, and mood tracking features designed to boost well-being, reduce stress, and promote positive emotional changes.

Moodfit

Helps you track your mood, set personal goals, and gain insights into your mental health, making it easier to understand and manage your emotional patterns.

MindShift CBT

Offers CBT-based strategies, practical tools, and mood tracking features to help manage anxiety and depression effectively.

Daylio

A digital mood journal that lets you effortlessly track your mood and daily activities, helping you identify patterns and triggers for better mental health management.
