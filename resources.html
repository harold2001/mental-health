Resources for Mental Well-being

National Institute of Mental Health (NIMH)

Offers evidence-based information on mental disorders, current research, and resources for finding help with depression, anxiety, and other conditions.

Anxiety and Depression Association of America (ADAA)

Provides educational articles, webinars, and practical tools for managing anxiety and depression.

Psych Central

Offers articles, self-assessments, and community forums to help users understand and manage various mental health issues.

7 Cups

Connects individuals with free, confidential emotional support through chat with trained listeners and offers affordable online therapy.

HelpGuide.org

Provides evidence-based self-help guides, practical advice, and articles for managing depression, anxiety, and other mental health challenges.
